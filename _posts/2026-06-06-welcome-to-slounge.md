## How to use this site
As mentioned in the original Jekyll documentation, you can easily add new content by creating files in the `_posts` directory. 

### Adding Images
To add your own images, place them in your `/assets/img/` folder and link to them like this:

`![Description](/assets/img/your-image-name.jpg)`
