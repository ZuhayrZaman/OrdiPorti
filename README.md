# Slounge 🛋️

**The unofficial student hub for LUT University, Lahti Campus.**

Welcome to the Slounge! This is your go-to digital space for navigating life at LUT University and settling into your new home in Lahti. Whether you’re trying to figure out the local bus routes, looking for the best student-friendly spots, or just want to survive your first Finnish winter, we’ve got you covered.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission
Moving to a new city for university is a big step. Slounge exists to bridge the gap between "new arrival" and "local expert" by providing practical, student-tested advice, guides, and tips. We’re here to make your transition smoother, less stressful, and a lot more fun.

## 📌 What You'll Find
- **Campus Essentials:** How to navigate the LUT campus, library resources, and student organizations.
- **Living in Lahti:** Grocery guides, transport tips (LSL), and the best local hangout spots.
- **Student Life:** Traditions, social events, and advice on balancing academics with downtime.
- **Survival Guides:** Everything from navigating the Finnish winter to finding affordable furniture.

## 🚀 Built by Students, for Students
Slounge is a community-driven, independent project. We believe the best advice comes from those who have lived it.
